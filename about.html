Our Mission

YAYTRAV is a domestic travel company located in Australia. The travel company is domestic in the sense that it provides services in all of Australia. The business is currently lacking in customers thus they are looking for ways to attract more customers. The main plan of the business is to get a new website to update their current website which was last updated in 2012.

Our Vission

The goal of the new travel website is to provide all essential information needed by customers to plan their perfect holiday trip/gateway for a variety of customers including Bride & Grooms wanting to go on a particular destination for a Wedding and/or Honeymoons, families who want to take a yearly vacation and baby boomers who are retired.

Goal

. The aims of the new website are to increase customers of the business by making services more accessible on the website, more attractive to customers through the inclusion of all website design principles resulting in a trustworthy feel of the website.

Our Model

The travel website is to promote the goal of the company which is will focus on its initial efforts in obtaining clients all around the world who are interested in luxury and general travel in Australia.
